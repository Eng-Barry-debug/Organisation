About Us

Kenyan Innovative Leaders Foundation (KILF_KENYA) empowers men and women for meaningful participation in governance, economy, and public affairs through our value-based leadership approach.

We are anchored by three program pillars:

  • Governance and Civic Engagement
  • Economic Empowerment
  • Leadership Development
